More About Yoni

Coming from a long background in dance, I have an appreciation for presentation, and take first impressions seriously. I have been teaching and performing various forms of Middle Eastern, North African, Turkic and Hellenistic dance styles since 2009. As a practitioner of so many styles, I noticed a throughcurrent of communication, and realized all styles have the potential to express similar concepts, and different people have different skill levels with different languages and messages, emphasizing the need for cooperation with teammates of different skillsets. My approach to design is to

  1. collaborate with others on the most important messages to deliver and the best languages to use
  2. repurpose that collective knowledge to remove all the clutter
  3. pair important information with a user-friendly structure and search-engine optimization
  4. streamline structure to focus on appearance and create a design that helps you stick out

I firmly believe that by following this process, I can work with a team of other developers to create an end product that satisfies all the needs a project could have, and that satisfies
